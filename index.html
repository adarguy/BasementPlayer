What is Basement Player?

Basement Player is your online source for computer parts, gaming gear, and cryptocurrency mining rigs. We offer affordable tech gear of the highest quality and can equip you with pre-built models that are ready to go or give you a great price on unassembled parts if you get a kick out of building it yourself. We make it easy to find everything you're looking for on one site and we take great pride in making sure that our pricing makes tech gear as accessible as possible. We look forward to helping you get your hands on premium gear so that you can step up your gear game.
