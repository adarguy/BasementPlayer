What is Basement Player?

Basement Player is your online source for gaming gear, cryptocurrency mining equipment and general computer parts. We offer affordable tech gear of the highest quality and can equip you with pre-built models that require little to no setup or give you a great price on unassembled parts if you get a kick out of building things yourself. We make it easy to find everything you're looking for on one site and we take great pride in making sure that our pricing makes tech gear as accessible as possible. From gaming equipment, high quality computer parts to cryptocurrency mining rigs, we are a one stop shop for all your gaming, computer and cryptocurrency mining needs! Our products are built inhouse and, just like with any product, orders are shipped out the same or very next business day. We look forward to helping you get your hands on premium gear so that you can step up your gear game.
We have partnered with major wholesalers in the industry to provide high quality computer parts at competitive rates. Everything is built in house, so the savings will be passed on to you! Whether you're looking for gaming computers, cryptocurrency mining rigs, or individual parts for your own project, Basement Player has it all!
